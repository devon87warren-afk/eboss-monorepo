COMMON FAULT CODES:

Engine Faults:
- E001: Low oil pressure (Check oil level, pressure sensor, pump)
- E002: High coolant temperature (Check coolant, radiator, thermostat)
- E003: Overcrank (Engine failed to start)
- E004: Overspeed (Engine exceeded safe RPM)
- E005: Low coolant level (Check for leaks)

Generator Faults:
- G001: Overvoltage (AVR malfunction or light load)
- G002: Undervoltage (Heavy load or AVR issue)
- G003: Overfrequency (Governor issue or light load)
- G004: Underfrequency (Heavy load or governor issue)
- G005: Overcurrent (Overload or short circuit)

Thermal System Faults:
- T001: Low water flow (Check pump, valves, strainer)
- T002: High supply temperature (Reduce load or check flow)
- T003: Low supply temperature (Increase load or check heat exchanger)
